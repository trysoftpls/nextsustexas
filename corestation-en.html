Core Station

Identifies which room and bed are requesting help and sends the information to the wristbands. Equipped with an RYLR 998 for maximum efficiency in hospitals and a database that tracks which wristbands attended to which patients.

Component Description

RYLR998 Sends data to the Medicall-bands and receives the response
