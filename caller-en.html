Patient Caller

This device allows patients to quickly request help in hospitals and healthcare centers. When patients use it, a signal is sent to the central unit to alert the wristbands.

Component Description
Pushbutton Sends a signal
LED Turns on when the button is pressed
RJ45 cable Transmits the signal to the Core station
