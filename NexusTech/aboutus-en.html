About Us

NexusTech S.A.S., based in Córdoba, Argentina, is a for-profit company dedicated to the development of advanced technological solutions. Its main activity focuses on the production, commercialization, and provision of specialized services in the technological field, always with an emphasis on innovation and quality. As an organization, NexusTech S.A.S. seeks to create value through the implementation of cutting-edge technologies, offering products and services that optimize efficiency and communication across various sectors. The company is committed to local economic development, promoting research and skilled employment in the region.

Mission

To optimize and facilitate communication in hospital environments, increasing efficiency through the production and commercialization of the medical band, and leading the provincial market.

Vision

To dominate the national and international electronics market, developing high-quality products that transform, improve, and ease people's lives.

Science Fair

We were selected to exhibit at the 2024 Science Fair in Córdoba, an event where we introduced our product to the public for the first time. During this event, many people showed great interest in our product, including employees from the Córdoba Secretariat of Innovation.

TIC Week

After the interest we generated at the Science Fair, we were given a stand to exhibit at TIC Week, an event where many products and technologies are showcased. At this event, we had the opportunity to talk to many knowledgeable people in the field who were surprised by our product. We also had the chance to speak with the Secretary of Innovation, an incredible opportunity.

The Creators Contest

We participated in the Creators Contest, organized in Chile. This competition featured projects from all over Latin America, and although we didn't win, we reached the stage just before the semi-finals. This contest was a very important driver for the development of our product.

ONIET

Winners of the 1st place in the prototype competition, Health and Wellness category, where we competed against projects from all over the country. This was the most anticipated competition of the year, and it showed that all of our team's hard work paid off.
